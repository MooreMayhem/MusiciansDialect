MUSICIAN'S DIALECT TEAMS' message to you

To those who support

What is there to say other than thank you? We need each other to step ahead. Support all the artists on this page because they deserve it. We have so much unheard talent and this where you can find them.

Big shout out to you, the listener. Giving these artists just a listen is all you need to do to support and we appreciate it. Not only do we say Fuck The Industry, we say we are The Industry. We continue to prove that not everybody needs to get paid for an artist's track to be heard. You want your track to be heard? Send it to the Musician's Dialect e-mail address, find this under Contact Us. Feel free to make suggestions, this website is for you anyway.

This is South African HipHop at it's best. We going to find the best talent and give them to you on a silver platter. Tell everybody you know about this, be part of the movement. We give credit to all parties on material: from the rappers, beat makers, cover editors, the photographers all the way to the haters, lovers and everybody in between. South African artists stand up! It costs nothing to be on the website. You email me your track, I give it a listen and put it up. Be sure to contact me and I will contact you back.

Let's help each other be great, and to improve the quality of our music.
